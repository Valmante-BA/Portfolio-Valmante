# HR Turnover Analysis (Excel)

##  Business Objective
The goal of this project is to analyze employee attrition to identify key factors influencing turnover.  
This analysis helps HR teams understand retention issues, workforce risks, and areas requiring corrective actions.

##  Dataset
Source: HR Employee Attrition Dataset (Kaggle)  
The dataset contains 1,470 employee records and 34 HR variables including:
- Attrition
- Age
- Monthly Income
- Job Role
- Department
- Job Satisfaction
- Overtime
- Performance Rating
- Years at Company

##  Tools Used
- Excel (Pivot Tables, Data Cleaning, KPIs)
- Excel Charts
- HR KPIs

##  Key Analysis
- Overall attrition rate
- Attrition by:
  - Age group
  - Department
  - Job role
  - Salary level
  - Seniority
  - Job satisfaction
  - Distance from home
- Identification of high‑risk groups

##  Insights
- Departments/roles with highest turnover
- Patterns linked to satisfaction, salary, overtime, etc.
- Employee profiles most likely to leave

##  Recommendations
- improving compensation for at‑risk groups  
- reducing overtime  
- revising management practices  
- enhancing career development
